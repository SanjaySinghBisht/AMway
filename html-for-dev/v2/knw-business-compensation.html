Silver Producer

A Silver Producer is an ABO who has achieved in one month one of the following three scenarios:

Award volume of at least 12,500PV or

Award volume of 5,000PV with 1 personally/foster sponsored 21% group or

2 personally/foster sponsored 21% groups.

Gold Producer

A Silver Producer is an ABO who has achieved in one month one of the following three scenarios:

Award volume of at least 12,500PV or

Award volume of 5,000PV with 1 personally/foster sponsored 21% group or

2 personally/foster sponsored 21% groups.

Founders Achievement Award

Besides the GIP, Platinum DDs and above could also qualify for other incentive programmes such as the Founders Achievement Award (FAA).

The FAA programme was initiated by Amway's Co-founders, Jay Van Andel & Rich DeVos. These annual discretionary cash awards are presented to those ABOs who demonstrate extraordinary business development and exhibit high ethical business standards.

Eligibility for the Awards

The FAA is a special discretionary programme offered by the Amway Policy Board and is NOT part of the Amway Sales & Marketing Plan.

To participate, an ABO must:

  • Be a Diamond Bonus recipient
  • Have a minimum of 8.00 FAA Leg Credits

Please note that the FAA credit is limited to one leg per International market (either No.2 business or internationally sponsored leg). The International legs cannot be double-counted by the same ABO.

Award Pin Recognition

Recognition would be given to FAA qualifiers of 40, 50, 60 & 70 FAA credits whereby special award p65 00 FCA 40, FCA 50, FCA 60 and FCA 70 (FCA = Founders Crown Ambassador) would be presented. The qualifiers will receive a first-time award upon qualification and also the FAA amount upon re,ualification. (Please refer to the FAA schedule).

Amway Leadership Seminar (ALS)

Who is eligible to qualify for the Amway Leadership Seminar (ALS)?

All Platinum DDs and above who achieve their required target points and also meet the required conditions are eligible to qualify for the Amway Leadership Seminar (ALS).

What are these conditions ?

One of the conditions is the ethical business conduct of the ABOship, in accordance to the letter and spirit of the Amway Rules of Conduct and the Direct Sales Act of Malaysia (for Malaysia ABOs only).

Others include:

  • The Platinum DDs and above must have attended a DD Seminar within 6 months of qualification as Platinum DDs.
  • Accounts are kept in good order

Note that ABOs who have inherited an Amway business may not be invited.

Is the ALS a guaranteed part of the Amway Sales & Marketing plan?

The ALS is not part of the Amway Sales & Marketing Plan and is subject to change.

What are the qualifications for attendance of the ALS?

  • For re-qualified ABOs, points are accumulated within the fiscal year, from September to the following August.
  • For new Platinum and above, accumulation of ALS points starts from the 1st month of 21% Silver Producer qualification or September, whichever comes later, and for each subsequent month when 21% is achieved only. Once Platinum level is achieved, points will be earned every month irrespective of the Performance Bonus level.

How are the ALS points calculated?

There are 2 ways to earn ALS Points, i.e. through your Personal Group PV and the 4% Leadership Bonus that you receive when breaking-off a 21% group. 4% Leadership Bonus of other currencies will be converted to Ringgit Malaysia for calculation. The formulas are:

  • PV (including pass-up of non-21% volume from your SP/GP/Platinum DD) x 0.25 Co Performance Bonus %, AND
  • 4% Leadership Bonus (RM) x 1.125 x PV/BV ratio

# 0.30 for Singapore ABOs

What is PV/BV ratio?

The PV/BV ratio is not a fixed figure. Please call Regional Sales Department for the latest PV/BV ratio.

Malaysia 0.2632
Brunei 0.3479
Singapore 0.3479

What are the targets for qualification to the ALS?

1st Timer = 10,000 points
2nd Timer = 13,000 points
3rd Timer = 16,000 points
4th Timer = 19,000 points
5th Timer = 22,000 points
6th Timer & above = 25,000 points

Diamond need only to re-qualify for Diamond levels or achieve the required target points, whichever is applicable.

You may also earn ALS bonus points to qualify for the ALS. Please refer to the Sales Incentive Programme
