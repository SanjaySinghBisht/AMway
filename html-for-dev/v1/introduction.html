Amway Malaysia’s 40th Anniversary

We’re 40!

AMWAY Malaysia reaches the big 4-0 this year, and we want you to come celebrate this remarkable milestone with us. We’ve got a whole list of exciting activities lined up for the year as we commemorate the success we’ve achieved since we first set up shop in 1976. A huge chunk of our success is due the dedication and passion of everyone involved in the AMWAY business and is the inspiration for #AM40 Pioneers For Tomorrow.

Here’s just a taste of what’s to come in the following months:

  • ​Drop off your empty NUTRILITE containers into our one-of-a-kind Recycling Wall and watch our secret message reveal itself as the Wall gets filled up.
  • Grab your copy of the Special Edition AMAGRAM which will wow you with its stunning visuals and interesting historic facts
  • ​Don’t miss getting L.O.C. and SA8 in their limited-released Heritage Packaging.
  • ​Fancy high quality cooking? Come preview our renowned Queen Cookware & Wok during the spectacular 2016 National Convention and Product Expo weekend.
  • Stay tuned as we launch the NUTRILITE Make The World a Better Place Campaign.
  • NUTRILITE Trio Promotion. Why Trio? Cause good things come in threes.
  • Join in the fun and excitement of the BodyKey Carnival as we celebrate your journey to successful weight management.

    • That’s just a sneak peek at what we have in store for you. Check out our Page regularly as we reveal more details of the activities for each month. Let’s celebrate 40 years of achievements together and make #AM40 Pioneers For Tomorrow a great success!

*All events are subject to change.
